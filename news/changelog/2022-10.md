A relatively good month. Managed to get a few things done and even launched the dev site!

<!--more-->

## Dev Site
Setup a development site to push updates to first instead of them going straight to the main site. This will be more helpful later on in development to ensure features work on a "live" environment before going *actually* live. The dev site will be limited access.

## Newsletter
Setup the newsletter feature. Not sure yet what sort of news will be sent out, but users can now submit their email and receive posts in the future.

## Notes
Added the notes section. This allows a user to view, create, and edit a note. Would also like add share functionality, where other users could view a note but not edit it. A feature for later.

## Minor Updates
* Updated the home page to include certain scrolling behaviour when switching between different views (news post and login/register form).
* Gave the initial loading page a much needed makeover.
* Changed the format of the date on news posts.
